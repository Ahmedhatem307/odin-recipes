Pizza

Description

Making pizza is really easy. it's one of the most popular italian dishes around the world, and it can be made with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • salt
  • sugar
  • oil
  • sauce
  • cheese
  • toppings

Steps

  1. mix the flour, water and oil
  2. add salt and sugar
  3. leave the dough to rest for 1 hour
  4. roll the dough
  5. add the sauce cheese and your favourite toppings
  6. put the pizza in the oven for 15 minutes
