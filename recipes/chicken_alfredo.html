Chicken Alfredo

description

Chicken alfredo is a really famous dish from Rome, Italy made by cheif Alfredo which is made from fettuccine pasta and a rich parmesan cheese sauce.

Ingredients

  • fettuccine pasta
  • parmesan cheese
  • heavy cream
  • mushrooms(optional)
  • butter
  • garlic
  • seasoning
  • chicken breasts
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. boil the pasta in water with a lot of salt
  2. cut the chicken breasts into cubes
  3. fry the garlic in the olive oil
  4. add the chicken cubes
  5. add the cream
  6. add the cheese
  7. let it simmer until the sauce gets heavier
  8. drain the pasta and put it on the sauce then serve
