Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna, a beloved Italian classic, is a hearty and comforting dish that layers pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. The foundation of a traditional lasagna starts with a robust Bolognese sauce made from ground beef, onions, garlic, and tomatoes, simmered together with herbs like oregano and basil. This sauce is layered with lasagna noodles, which can be either boiled beforehand or used as no-boil sheets. Each layer is then generously covered with béchamel sauce—a creamy mixture of butter, flour, and milk—and a blend of cheeses, typically mozzarella and Parmesan, which melt beautifully to create a gooey, golden top.

Assembling the lasagna involves spreading a thin layer of meat sauce at the bottom of the baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, more meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese. This process is repeated until the dish is filled, ending with a final layer of noodles topped with the remaining béchamel and cheese. Baked in the oven until the top is bubbly and golden brown, lasagna is a perfect example of comfort food, offering a delightful mix of flavors and textures in every bite. Whether served for a family dinner or a special occasion, this dish is sure to please and satisfy with its rich, layered goodness.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 4 cups milk
  • Pinch of nutmeg
  • 12 lasagna noodles (boiled or no-boil)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese (optional, can be used in place of or along with béchamel)
  • 1 large egg (optional, to mix with ricotta for creamier texture)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat
  2. Sauté the Vegetables
  3. Simmer the Sauce
  4. Make the Roux
  5. Add the Milk
  6. Preheat the Oven
  7. Boil the Noodles (if using boiled noodles)
  8. Layer the Ingredients
  9. Bake the Lasagna
  10. Rest and Serve
