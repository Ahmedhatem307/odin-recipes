Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular italian dish that contains a lot of ingredients and is somewhat difficult to make, but it's really delicious!

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • canned tomatoes
  • onions
  • ground beef
  • olive oil
  • milk
  • flour
  • butter
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. heat the olive oil and cook the onions until they are soft
  2. add the ground beef
  3. add the canned tomatoes after the beer has been cooked
  4. let it simmer for 2 hours
  5. make a dux from the butter and flour
  6. add the milk to make the bechamel sauce
  7. put the noodle and two sauces on each other
  8. put in the oven for 30 minutes or until it's golden brown
